Solar Power Uganda Ltd is a service company dealing in sourcing, supplying, installation and servicing of solar PV systems, solar water heaters, power backup systems, streetlights, water pumping solutions and electrical works. Our network of reputable international manufacturers ensures that the products and services offered to the community are of the best performance and quality standards; as such, honesty, trust, and expertise have won us a good reputation across the region as a contractor of choice for solar & electrical energy solutions.
